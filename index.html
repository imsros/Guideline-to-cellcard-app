Cellcard Company Profile

CamGSM PLC is Cambodia's longest-serving Telecommunications Company with more than 20 years of operation and a customer base of three million subscribers. The company launched in 1997 in a joint venture with Millicom International Cellular offering customers the first prepaid service with scratch cards.Operating under the name Mobitel, the company had always been the market leader bringing innovation in both technology and customer service.

Who is Cellcard today?

Cellcard is Proudly Khmer. Its current strapline framing all current communications and compaigns is that Cellcard is for Everyone Everywhere. This reinforces the strategy of affordable access to mobile data for all in Cambodia and the company's steps to evolve the market to an Always Digital Lifestyle and Economy .

3. Register

If your phone number didn't verify with your national identifier yet :

  1. Send a photo of your valid ID with your phone number to our Facebook page:Cellcard SIM Registration.
  2. E-mail a photo of your valid ID to: customercare@cellcard.com.kh.
  3. Register online at Cellcard website: www.cellcard.com.kh/en/support

If you already verified your phone number with your nation identifier :

  • Open your cellcard app on your device.
  • Fill your phone number then they will send you a code vai your phone number.
  • Then fill that code, you'll get into your account
